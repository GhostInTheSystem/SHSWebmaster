Commercial drones have seen an increase in popularity since the late 2010s. Since 2005, the Federal Aviation Authorities has issued 78 certificates to commercial drones. By 2012, the FAA had reportedly issued more than 300 drone licenses. The FAA has had quite the history with commercial drones. FAA issues limitations for commercial drone use, but has had enough pressure to authorize the use of commercial drones.The agency has continued slowly, but has continued nonetheless. With commercial drones being increasing popular, drones will certainly populate the sky in the near future.
